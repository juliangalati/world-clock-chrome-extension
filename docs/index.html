World Clock – Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Overview

World Clock is a Chrome extension that displays the current time in one or more time zones. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is used.

Data we collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension does not communicate with any external server.

Data stored locally

The extension stores the following in chrome.storage.sync:

  • Your selected time zones and display preferences

This data is stored by Chrome and, if you are signed into Chrome, may be synced across your own devices by Google. It is never sent to us or any third party.

Permissions

  • alarms – used to refresh the clock display at regular intervals.
  • storage – used to save your time zone preferences between sessions.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
